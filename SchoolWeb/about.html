About Unique Assurance

Shaping Future Leaders Since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Unique Assurance School Complex began with a vision to provide quality, affordable education to children in Kumasi and its environs. What started as a small creche has now grown into a comprehensive educational institution serving students from Creche through Senior High School.

Our founder, Mrs. Grace Mensah, envisioned a school where every child would receive individual attention and holistic development. She started with just 15 students and 3 teachers, driven by a passion for education and a belief in nurturing young minds.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that develops students academically, morally, and socially, preparing them to become responsible citizens and future leaders who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Ghana recognized for excellence in academic achievement, character development, innovation, and producing well-rounded individuals ready for global challenges.

Our Journey

2010

Foundation Year

Unique Assurance School was founded with just a creche facility, starting with 15 students and 3 dedicated teachers.

2012

Expansion to Nursery

Expanded our programs to include nursery education, growing our student population to 50 children.

2015

Primary School Launch

Started Primary School program with first batch of 30 students and state-of-the-art facilities.

2018

JHS Accreditation

Received full accreditation for Junior High School program from Ghana Education Service.

2020

SHS Program Launch

Launched Senior High School program with Science, Business, and General Arts options.

2024

Modern Campus

Completed new modern campus with science labs, computer labs, and sports facilities.

Meet Our Founder

Mrs. Grace Mensah

Founder & Headmistress

With over 25 years of experience in education, Mrs. Grace Mensah founded Unique Assurance School Complex with a vision to create an institution that would nurture young minds and prepare them for future challenges.

Her educational philosophy centers on holistic development, believing that every child deserves quality education combined with moral and character building.

"Education is not just about academic excellence, but about shaping character, building confidence, and preparing young minds to become responsible global citizens."

Qualifications

  • M.Ed Educational Leadership (University of Cape Coast)
  • B.Ed Arts (University of Education, Winneba)
  • Certificate in Child Psychology
  • Member, Ghana National Association of Private Schools
