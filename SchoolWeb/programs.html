Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours for different programs?

Creche & Nursery: 7:30 AM - 4:00 PM
Primary School: 7:30 AM - 3:30 PM
JHS & SHS: 7:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Extra-curricular activities run until 5:00 PM

What is the admission process?

1. Submit completed application form with required documents
2. Attend interview/assessment (where applicable)
3. Receive admission offer letter
4. Pay required fees to secure placement
5. Attend orientation before school starts

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we offer safe and reliable transportation services covering major routes in Kumasi and surrounding areas. Our buses are equipped with GPS tracking, safety belts, and are driven by certified drivers with trained attendants.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including: Sports (football, basketball, athletics), Music & Dance, Drama Club, Debate & Public Speaking, ICT Club, Science Club, French Club, and Community Service projects.

How do you ensure student safety?

We have comprehensive safety measures including: 24/7 security personnel, CCTV surveillance, controlled access points, regular safety drills, first-aid trained staff, secure transportation, and strict visitor protocols.
